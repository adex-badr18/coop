About Us

Gwarzo Road Teachers' Pristine Multipurpose Cooperative Society Limited (GRT-PRISTINE) is a non-interest based cooperative society for the general public to satisfy the needs of the Muslim Ummah.
The society is registered with Kano State Ministry of Commerce with registration no KN44687.

Learn More

Objectives and benefits include:

  • To provide savings facilities to its members.
  • To create a source of credit for its members at zero interest rate.
  • To improve the quality of life of its members.
  • To enable its members manage their financial resources for mutual benefits.

Eligibility Criteria

To become a member of this noble society and start benefiting from series of financial services, you must possess all of the following criteria

  • 01 Religion

    You must be a muslim of reputable character.

  • 02Adult

    You must be an adult of not less than eighteen (18) years of age.

  • 03 State of Residence

    You must be a resident of Kano State.

Services

We offer financial products and services that improves the quality of life of our members.

Target Savings

Members can engage in target savings for specific needs such as hajj/umrah, school fees, house rent, partake in investments, and so on.

Consumer Facilities

Members can order for facilities such as regular consumables, special commodity request, ramadan needs, eid-ul-fitr/eid-ul-kabir needs, etc.

Islamic Financial Products

Members can engage in islamic services such as investment partnership (mudharabah), cost-plus financing (murabaha), benevolent lending (qard) and joint enterprise (musharaka).
